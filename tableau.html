Birth Rates

Importance

    With the Birthrate of many countries decreasing, or stagnated at less than 1.5%, a sharp decrease in population will occur when the older generations start to pass away. This Tableau allows us to see that this problem affects more countries than we think, and that there is a problem for the generations to come.
